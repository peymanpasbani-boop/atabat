Status Legend
Pending — permission requests waiting for a response.
Primary Approved — permissions that are primarily accepted.
Request Cancelled — cancellation requested by the airline.
Primary Cancelled — cancellation primarily approved by CAO.
Final Cancelled — cancellation finally approved by CAO.
Primary Denied — requests primarily rejected by CAO.
Final Denied — requests thoroughly rejected by CAO.
Final Approved — requests finally approved by CAO.
